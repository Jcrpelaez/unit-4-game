Stone Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of game. There are four power stones below. By clicking on a stone you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching the total score to the random number, you lose if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each stone is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of the stone.
